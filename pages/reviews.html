Our review

Cliffside Views

Breathtaking ocean views line several holes, offering a coastal backdrop that makes every round unforgettable. The South Course’s scenic cliffs are as iconic as its layout.

Smooth Car Paths

Cart-friendly from first tee to 18th green. The paths are well-maintained and accessible, perfect for players who want comfort without missing the action.

Championship-Level Challenge

Host of the U.S. Open and PGA Tour events, Torrey Pines South is a true test of skill. Long par 4s, demanding greens, and wind off the ocean guarantee a memorable round.
